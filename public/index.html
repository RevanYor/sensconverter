Finding Your Perfect Sensitivity

PSA Method

Perfect Sensitivity Approximation - A systematic approach

  1. 1. Start with a very high sensitivity (where you overaim)
  2. 2. Then try a very low sensitivity (where you underaim)
  3. 3. Pick a sensitivity in the middle of these two
  4. 4. Repeat until you find your comfortable range
  5. 5. Fine-tune within that range

DPI Settings

Recommended range: 400 - 1600 DPI

  • • Keep the same DPI across all games
  • • Use your mouse's native DPI steps

Key Guidelines

Essential tips for better aim consistency

  • • One full 360° turn should take about 30-40cm of mouse movement
  • • Lower sensitivity means better precision but requires more arm movement
  • • Consider your available mousepad space
  • • Stick with your chosen sensitivity for at least a week

Common Mistakes

Avoid these common sensitivity pitfalls

  • • Changing sensitivity too frequently
  • • Copying pro players exactly
  • • Using different sensitivities across similar games
  • • Not considering your natural arm movement range
