Finding Your Perfect Sensitivity

PSA Method

Perfect Sensitivity Approximation - A systematic approach:

  1. Start with a very high sensitivity (where you overaim).
  2. Then try a very low sensitivity (where you underaim).
  3. Pick a sensitivity in the middle of these two.
  4. Repeat until you find your comfortable range.
  5. Fine-tune within that range.

DPI Settings

Recommended range: 400 - 1600 DPI

  • Keep the same DPI across all games.
  • Use your mouse's native DPI steps.

Key Guidelines

Essential tips for better aim consistency:

Common Mistakes

Avoid these common sensitivity pitfalls:

  • Changing sensitivity too frequently.
  • Copying pro players exactly.
  • Using different sensitivities across similar games.
  • Not considering your natural arm movement range.
